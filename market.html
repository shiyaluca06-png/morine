(Japan market strategy / branding / visual direction)

We help brands shape a clearer direction before entering a new market. From brand positioning and visual tone to Japan-focused market research, we support brands in understanding how their products may be received, how their image can be refined, and which direction may feel more natural for local consumers, buyers, and stores. This service is suitable for brands that want to explore Japan or overseas markets but are not yet sure how to present themselves.
